This recipe is a complete and utter Caitlin originalTM - she came up with the recipe all by herself. I love Caitlin and I love these beans and she loves that I love them because they are pretty easy to make and delicious. Like all great Caitlin recipes, all measurements add instructions are vibes based.

Ingredients

  • Shallot
  • Lemon, cut in half
  • Garlic (as much as your heart desires)
  • Salt (also as much as your heart desires)
  • 2 tins of white beans (like butter or cannelinni)
  • Several tsps of dried herbs (italian herb mix, oregano, basil etc)
  • 1L of stock (made of 1L of water and 3 tsp of vegeta powder)
  • Half a cup of risoni
  • Parm

Method

  1. Fry off the shallot, lemon (cut side down) and garlic in olive oil until slightly charred. Season(with salt)
  2. Add 2 cans of beans, herbs and stock.
  3. Boil until broth is brothy.
  4. Add risoni and cook for 8 mins.
  5. Serve with freshly grated parm.
