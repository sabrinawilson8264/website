I take absolutely no credit for this recipe whatsoever - the original creator is Zaynab Paruk. I came accross this recipe on tiktok and have been making it for many years due to its use of pantry ingredients and lack of fussiness. I am writing it down here as I no longer have tiktok and can't refer back to the original video.
I recommend eating with coconut rice and plain yogurt.

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp garlic
  • 1 tsp ginger
  • 1 tsp each of ground cumin, coriander, garam masala, and salt
  • 1/2 tsp each of chilli flakes and tumeric
  • 1 cup (or more) of veggie stock
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 tin of crushed tomatos
  • 1.5 cups of dry red lentils

Method

  1. Start by heating up some olive oil in a saucepan. Fry garlic, ginger and herbs for a few mins until fragrant
  2. Add water, stock, toms and lentils
  3. Bring to a simmer, stirring so nothing burns on the bottom. Simmer until lentils are all nice and soft, adding more water or stock when needed.
