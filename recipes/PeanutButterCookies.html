Ingredients

  • 1 cup of Peanut Butter
  • 1 egg
  • 2/3 cup of sugar (or to taste)
  • 1 tspn bicarb soda
  • 100g of chocolate (either chips or cut up into chunks)
  • Big pinch of salt

Method

  1. Mix ingredients together
  2. Roll into balls (or scoop with a spoon?) (how many?)
  3. Bake at 180 degrees C for 10 mins
  4. Share =)
